Common Questions

FAQs

Is it free to register as a worker?
Yes — registering on Khaya is completely free for workers. The only costs involved are for the actual background check and criminal clearance certificate, which we help facilitate at the most affordable rates possible.
How long does the vetting process take?
Basic vetting (Tier 1) typically takes 5–10 working days depending on how quickly the criminal check is processed. We'll keep you updated every step of the way via WhatsApp.
How much does it cost employers to use Khaya?
Employers pay a placement fee per successful hire. We also offer a monthly subscription for employers making frequent placements. Full pricing is available on our Find Talent page.
What happens if a worker doesn't work out?
We offer a replacement guarantee within a defined period. If a placed worker doesn't work out for legitimate reasons, we will rematch you at no additional cost.
Are workers protected on Khaya?
Absolutely. All contracts are BCEA-compliant, we help with UIF registration, and employers on our platform are rated by workers too. Exploitative behaviour gets flagged and investigated.
Which cities are you currently operating in?
We are launching in Johannesburg and surrounding areas first — including Sandton, Midrand, Pretoria, and the East and West Rand. Cape Town and Durban will follow.

Every single worker on Khaya goes through a structured, multi-layer vetting process. This is not a checkbox exercise — it's a genuine commitment to the safety of employers, children, and vulnerable people across South Africa.
