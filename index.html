Fair pay, in the open

What's a fair wage?

We publish honest rate guides so workers aren't lowballed and families know what's fair. Every rate sits above the national minimum wage of R30.23/hour.

Nanny / au pairR40–R150/hr
Cleaner / housekeeperR35–R100/hr
GardenerR35–R120/hr
Elderly carerR50–R250/hr
DriverR40–R300/hr
Pet careR40–R150/hr
Home fixer / tradeR80–R350/hr
Minimum wage (legal floor)R30.23/hr

These are typical hourly wages the worker earns — separate from Khaya's once-off placement fee. Final pay is always agreed between you and the worker, in a proper BCEA contract.

The laws we follow
