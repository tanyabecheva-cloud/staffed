# Khaya — Brand Guide

> **khaya** *(isiZulu / isiXhosa, noun)* — "home."
> The whole product is *who you let into your home*. The name is the promise.

## Positioning
**The trust of a top placement agency, the ease of an app — for everyone you let into your home.**

Khaya is South Africa's vetted home & care workforce platform: nannies, domestic workers,
gardeners, elderly carers, drivers, home maintenance and pet care — every person
background-checked, ID-verified, reference-called, and rated.

## Tagline
- **Primary:** Trust, brought home.
- Worker-facing: Work with dignity.
- Functional: Vetted. Verified. Yours.

## The two-sided story
- **Employer side = trust.** Who is coming into my home, near my kids, my gran?
- **Worker side = hope/dignity.** Fair contracts, UIF help, employers rated too.
- Colour carries the story: **blue = trust, gold = hope.**

## Type
- **Display / wordmark:** Playfair Display (700) — warm, human, trustworthy serif.
- **Body / UI:** DM Sans — clean, legible, modern.
- **Wordmark:** lowercase `khaya` + a gold full-stop `.` (the dot carries the hope colour).

## Voice
Warm, plain, dignified. No corporate hedging, no "synergy." Talk to a real Joburg parent
and a real domestic worker in the same breath. Lead with safety, close with dignity.

## Logo
- Wordmark: `khaya.` (Playfair 700, Trust Blue, gold dot).
- Mark: handshake-in-circle (inherited) — **flag for redesign** to a simpler home/door + sunrise
  mark in phase 2. Current mark is acceptable for MVP launch.
