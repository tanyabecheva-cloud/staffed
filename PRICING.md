# Khaya — Pricing Model

## Principle
**Workers pay nothing. Employers pay.** Revenue never comes from a cut of a worker's
wage (that would contradict the "no exploitation" brand and choke supply). All revenue is
employer-side. Workers only ever cover the *at-cost* price of their own criminal clearance,
which Khaya helps facilitate (or fronts and recoups in the placement fee).

## The model: placement fee + optional subscription

### 1. Placement fee (one-off, paid only on successful hire)
| Tier | Roles | Price* | Includes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | Cleaners, gardeners, drivers, pet care | **R1,499** | AFISwitch/SAPS criminal check, Home Affairs ID + reference checks, BCEA contract, first-week check-in, **90-day replacement guarantee** |
| **Enhanced** | Nannies, au pairs, elderly carers | **R2,499** | Everything in Standard + sex-offender register check, skills assessment, video interview |

\* Validate against willingness-to-pay. Anchor: traditional agencies charge R2,500–R8,000
(often "one month's salary"). Khaya undercuts them while offering more (tech + guarantee).

### 2. Khaya Care (optional monthly subscription) — **~R149/month**
Ongoing check-ins (both sides), monthly payroll & UIF admin, contract updates/renewals,
priority rematch. This is the **recurring-revenue engine** and monetises the check-in service.

### 3. Workers — **free**
Free to apply, get verified, find work. Pay only the ~R300 clearance cost at cost. No wage cut.

## Why this shape
- **Pay-on-success** = low friction, aligned, familiar to employers from agencies.
- **Subscription** = recurring revenue (makes the business investable) + monetises check-ins.
- **Free workers** = supply moat + brand integrity.

## The risk: disintermediation
Employer + worker connect, then go off-platform to dodge fees. Mitigate by:
1. Charge the placement fee **before** contact details are exchanged.
2. Make Khaya Care genuinely worth keeping (real UIF/payroll/admin hassle removed).
3. Tie the replacement guarantee to staying on-platform.

## Unit economics (rough, per Standard placement)
- Revenue: R1,499. Cost: criminal+ID check ~R335 + ops/matching time. Gross margin healthy.
- Real upside is **Khaya Care attach rate** — every subscriber is recurring margin.

## To validate before scaling
Willingness-to-pay at these prices, Khaya Care attach rate, disintermediation leakage %,
and whether Enhanced's deeper vetting cost still leaves margin at R2,499.
